What is plasma therapy? Why you should care.

Plasma Therapy is method of fighting viral infections is by using plasma (or seraa) from recovered patients and inserting them into severe and critical patients. The plasma from recovered patients carries antibodies created naturally to fight the disease. This is an established treatment strategy in other viral infections, hence it is being tested for SARS COVID-19 as well.

This plasma, which contains the antibodies to the Corona SARS-2 virus, is injected to an ill person with COVID-19, they will do what they are destined to do: knock off the virus, and thus help in the recovery of this patient. This has been done with Ebola, and a couple of other viruses. Indian Council of Medical Research is at the moment asked hospitals and laboratories to test whether this will be viable for COVID-19 as well.









What is the purpose of this website?

This website is a platform which solves a problem of connectivity. Indian Council for Medical Research, on 14th April 2020 declared that laboratories and hospitals across India can now start testing for convalescent plasma therapy while treating severe COVID 19 patients. Convalescent plasma therapy is a well established method in medical sciences while treating patients of viral diseases.

  • Recovered COVID19 patients sign up as Donors.
  • Hospitals which require plasma samples also sign up.
  • Hospitals are able to locate Donors in their vicinity and contact.
  • Sensitive information about the donors is not publicly released.
